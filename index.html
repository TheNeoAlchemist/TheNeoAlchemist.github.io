How It Works

Nanostructured gold thin-films change colour upon target binding.
Colour changes too subtle for the eye are captured digitally and analysed by proprietary algorithms (inspired by astronomical adaptive optics).

Only molecules within a 20–40 nm sensing range contribute to signal — no wash step required.
PAT™ (Plasmonic Amplification Technology) enhances signal with proprietary moiety-conjugates, delivering high signal without excess background.
